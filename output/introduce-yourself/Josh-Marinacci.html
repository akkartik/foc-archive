# Josh Marinacci 2019-06-17 13:46
Hello everyone.
I found Steve Krouse’s podcast, and then to this Slack. In my day job I’m a developer evangelist for Mixed Reality at Mozilla, but I’ve had programming languages and usability as a passion of mine for many years. A lot stems from my belief that the best technology is something which empowers people to solve their own problems. The computer was supposed to be “the bicycle for the mind”, and yet it’s getting less hackable and customizable over time. There is such enormous potential in providing programmability to people who are experts in something other than programming.

Also, I think there has to be a better way to program computers than ascii text stored on disk. Surely when we build the Starship Enterprise in 300 years it won’t still have a command line in it. Right? :slightly_smiling_face:
