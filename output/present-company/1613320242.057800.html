# Cole Lawrence 2021-02-15 09:01
@Martin Sosic I've heard of Wasp lang, it seems like a pretty great project!
Many reasons for trialing F# during this phase. It's mostly because it shares a lot of similarities with Rust (which we use heavily), and it has great support in VS Code (which we use mainly since we also use a lot of TypeScript).
I really like the sorbet ecosystem, and believe that F# has a better "works out of the box" feel than languages like Haskell or OCaml. The compatibility with C# is awesome as well to leverage a huge existing ecosystem (maybe: depending on .Net 5 compat).
Honestly, Haskell also feels really intimidating and seems to have a fractured ecosystem of different language extensions. But, that's just my early take on things. I'll certainly hold stronger opinions after spending more time with FSharp
