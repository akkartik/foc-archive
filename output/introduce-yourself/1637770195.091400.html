I guess I will say a word or two about the project, other than getting the “vibe” from the teaser…

This project represents an attempt to work around the fact that our de facto window onto the network of people and information is a passive media consumption device -- the web browser (it’s great and all but that’s what it is in it’s current/historical form).

What I’m after is a visual and interactive tool that is also an evolving information system.

There is a bit of ZUIs, a touch of Smalltalk spirit, more than a sprinkling of various hypertext ideas, full text search, planning and automation etc all written in Clojure / Clojurescript.
