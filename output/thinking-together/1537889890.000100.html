# Ivan Reese 2018-09-25 15:07
What's much more interesting to me is the relationship between hydraulic, pneumatic, and electrical systems, and the schematic representations of those systems. Schematics aren't a computer programming language (or, perhaps more accurately, configuration language) the way ladder logic is, but they are very interesting ways of representing dynamic, intricate systems in a visual medium.
# Ivan Reese 2018-09-25 15:10
