# cauli 2020-01-09 01:03
i18n.__('Hello') suggests to me that the strategy here is the "gettext" way in which the translations are extracted from the codebase then replaced in runtime, and key of a translation is a original string in the en version of it.
# cauli 2020-01-09 01:06
It sounds weird to me that you are using graphql to query for each bit of text. You should have files that consolidate all translations for a locale for your app/system and then fetch them all at once (the most appropriate translations file), depending on the user's locale.
