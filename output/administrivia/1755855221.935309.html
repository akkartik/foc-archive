# Ivan Reese 2025-08-23 12:09
Re op: yes, these changes are intentionally funny.

Re "what's the point": fun!

In any event, I've changed it to "Feeling of Computing", which is the name we'll be going with henceforth, since it better reflects the values I'd like the community to embody. Hopefully this change provides a bit of invigoration, in addition to the brief period of confusion. It'll be okay.

I'm going to gradually switch stuff over to the new name, so apologies for any old/new side-by-side for the next bit.

Re "insiders club": when the community was first exploding in popularity (2020-ish), the people who inferred what "future of coding" meant from the name often got it wrong — more often than not, in my estimation. We had to do quite a bit of work to help newcomers learn what this space is for. I don't think any name would make that work needless, but I believe the new name will make it easier.
