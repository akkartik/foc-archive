# Beni Cherniavsky-Paskin 2023-08-28 10:53
Snap! removes most limitations of Scratch, has lambdas and everything, is really "Scheme in sheep's clothing".
The paradigm of of "tell object X to execute this piece of code in its scope" is imho a nice stepping stone to OOP and state management without the formalities of encapsulation & named methods. (OOP purists might disagree?)

I've been using NetsBlox which is fork of Snap! with multiplayer game support. Havent really got multiplayer to work yet, and i found the IDE a little buggy.
