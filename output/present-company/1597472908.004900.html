# Ivan Reese 2020-08-14 23:28
Welcome to #present-company!

This is a brand-new channel. It's our home for discussions about the present world — how we do programming today, how the world as it exists influences us, and generally any thoughts or questions about the here and now.

If you'd like help with your homework, this is the place to ask. If you wrote a new blog post about how to use react-thingamabob, this is the place to share it.

• Reflections on how the current world might influence the future go in #thinking-together — really, any thoughts about the future go there, even if they draw on the present or history.

# Ivan Reese 2020-08-15 06:29
Hah, nice. We might be able to make something of that during the holiday season :p

Present company is part of a figure of speech. Here's an example of how it's usually used: Let's say this is some high fantasy setting, and we're in a party together. I'm an elf, and you're a human. You might say outwardly "I've never met an elf I could respect," then looking at me, "present company excepted."

It's "company" in the sense of the people around you — both in the future of speech and as used for this channel. The word "present", then, has a double meaning for us — it's meant to mean both "here" as in we are all in this place, and "now" as in this is the channel where we talk about matters of the present day.

This is the channel where we all discuss matters of the here and now, drawing on the expertise and breadth of experience that this community has brought together.

Our other name ideas for this channel were all too narrow in their scope – eg: #present-of-programming didn't leave the door open to discussions of slightly more off-topic things, which is something I want this channel to be available for. I also wanted to put the focus on the fact that the people in this community are all alive in the present and there's a value to us all being together.
