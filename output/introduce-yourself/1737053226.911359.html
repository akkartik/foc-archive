Hey everyone! I'm Jeff. Thought I'd try a new online community and this seemed like a good one. Like a lot of you, I assume, I think about this topic a lot. Maybe too much. Some notable background: back in the day I coined and popularized webhooks to try and make the web more programmable. I created a bunch of stuff to encourage and support adoption including a free tool called localtunnel that a friend turned into a company called Ngrok, if anybody's used that.
