Speaking of interfaces, this video of various Mac team members introducing MacPaint and MacWrite (with rapturous audience reactions) is priceless. Especially delightful is the Q&A, which starts around halfway through the video. Through the video, you get a good sense of a few things:
• Features of MacWrite and MacPaint you might not have figured out for yourself when playing with them as a 5 year old.
• What particular aspects of these apps were surprising / delightful to a very tech-savvy audience.
• What particular aspects of these apps are seen as "the thing" by various Mac team members
• What sorts of technical and design tradeoffs the team made (becomes especially apparent in the Q&A)
