Saw the catalogue post on HN and found it super interesting :slightly_smiling_face: I'm one of the cofounders of https://withkoji.com , a web-based IDE + hosting platform trying to make it easier for non-devs and junior devs to create games and web apps (JS or JS-flavored things) by remixing existing apps and then using a combination of code and no-code interfaces to create something new. We're fresh out of beta, and already have a ton of different people from all around the world and all walks of life creating all kinds of weird things -- the goal is to do to app development what YouTube did to video publishing. Some more info here: https://venturebeat.com/2019/04/23/gometa-raises-6-million-a… and here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BgpFGMH6VR4 (long technical video). Anyway, excited to have found this community and look forward to participating more!
