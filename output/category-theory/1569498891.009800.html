IE, the first thing we do when defining a category is choose the objects, describe some base relationships, and describe a way to compose those relationships.
That basically gives us a way to calculate all the relationships that could possibly exist between those objects.
All the rest of CT so far has been "properties" that describe and/or predict what relationships do or don't exist
