# Ivan Tam 2020-07-27 22:10
@nicolas decoster in Jeskola Buzz you lay out signal flows in a graph (nodes and edges). generators send sound through filters and eventually to an audio output node. The generators themselves are sequenced with a built-in tracker. It was a loose hybrid of Max/MSP and trackers.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ReBk2WFliig

It hasn’t been actively developed in years. I don’t recall it being available on platforms other than Windows.

It would say Sunvox is the contemporary incarnation of Buzz: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iUx8PrDqUiY
