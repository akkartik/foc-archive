# Mike Travers 2020-01-25 10:39
Yes, the idea is to make the data accessible to our non-coding biologists. It also is designed to help people become coders, so it displays the generated text Datalog query (in Clojure/Datomic form, and also in a R Datalog dialect called Wick used locally).
