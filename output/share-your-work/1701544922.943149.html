Hi All
I’m almost “done” with my VR visual/spatial programming app, Pebble Graphics :tada: (“done” because I still see a lot of things that could be improved, but there are other projects I’m excited to start so I’m shipping what I have). It’s a VR version of turtle graphics with some interactive program stepping and 3D visualizations of things like state change and execution history.
I made a trailer you can watch (and listen to :notes:) here https://youtu.be/Fl3DjzTdUo8?si=bWi7CWwbXIQh_wUw. If you have a Quest headset and want to try it out, I set up a Beta release while I try to get it into Meta’s App Lab app store. This link https://www.meta.com/s/2Rlda8ess should work otherwise feel free to DM me the email address associated with your Oculus/Meta account.
I also have some time-stamped/annotated videos later on in this Youtube Playlist (https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLhmEEtPzG7mW4JrBUhwbo3al6HjaJsDp2 ) in case you want to see more.
I’m more of a lurker in this slack, but it’s a community I enjoy checking on and it’s really cool to see it grow!
