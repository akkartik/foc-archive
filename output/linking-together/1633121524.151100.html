# Chris Knott 2021-10-17 11:41
I have access to the Alpha now and have given it the briefest play around.

Basically it is very similar to Notion. In fact some aspects are so similar they might run into some legal trouble tbh.

Given that Notion's main negative for me is that it's always online and hosted on their servers, I would say that this implementation is much better, but from a UX experience they are pretty similar with Anytype missing quite a lot of features at the moment. It's very early days and they don't have Notion's resources I don't think, so a direct comparison isn't really fair, but the main distinguishing feature is how they are implemented, not what they do.

I was impressed with the team who gave the demo, they are very down to earth, open to suggestions, honest about limitations etc.
