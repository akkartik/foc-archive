# Roben Kleene 2019-06-10 10:37
I use https://www.spacious.com It's great for the price but not so great for meeting people (since it's first come first serve, so different people sitting around you everyday). If you try it out, I recommend visiting one of their dedicated locations rather than the restaurants. You can tell the dedicated ones because they're all open till 8 PM.
# Roben Kleene 2019-06-10 10:37
I've also had a great experience with https://letsensemble.com
# Roben Kleene 2019-06-10 10:39
I prefer Ensemble to WeWork because they have a far better experience for dedicated desks, e.g., a permanent desk in an open space, great for spontaneous conversations. WeWork dedicated desk (that I've seen) are in weird glass cubes.
