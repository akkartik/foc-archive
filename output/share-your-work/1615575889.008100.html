There's a final video showcase coming up on the 18th, which is somewhere between a pitch & presentation from each member in the cohort. You're welcome to watch it yourself, and I'd really appreciate it if you could link it to any contacts you have with:
• companies with 1-20 C/C++ programmers
• computer science lecturers/teachers
• anyone with experience in bringing software to market, particularly in the games industry
(obviously only if you think they might be interested, don't spam people :smile:)

The full showcase is on the 18th at 12pm GMT, with a Q&A at 12:55. My 7-minute slot starts at 12:33 if you're not available for the whole thing.

You can sign up for it at either of these links:
