# Lu Wilson 2022-08-26 02:19
Hi everyone! I'm Lu (or Luke - either is fine) and I use the @Lu Wilson handle in most places. For around 7 years, I was a teacher for young kids with additional needs - but in January I left that to dedicate more time to coding. I'm currently working as a freelance developer+designer!

I really like expressing myself with creative coding, and I share my stuff in some slightly-surreal videos. I enjoy making unusual programming languages+systems that let you think about things visually+spatially. I'm also helping to make a drag-and-drop tool for making cellular automata called Sandspiel Studio.

And I'm going to the next London meetup! Come say hello! :)
