# Andrew F 2024-03-26 20:54
In defense of the question: files and processes are not only pervasive in the present reality we need to deal with, they are so pervasive that any feasible future of computing will at least have to work alongside them, and most likely will be implemented on top of them (at least in part), because fully replacing them is such an incredible amount of work that it's going to happen over decades at best. Anyone involved in the near future of coding needs to understand them.

Are people really going to try to say you shouldn't try to understand the layers under you, or past attempts at problems we want to solve? That's not what I expect from this community.
