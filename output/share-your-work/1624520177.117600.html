One of two talks from this year's ClojureD (German Clojure conference) about work with which I've been helping the team at Nextjournal. One thing we don't talk about here is that in the future the command subsystem will also be expandable and scriptable by end users. There's more Lisp Machine-related goodness coming in the next talk video, which should be released in the coming days. :slightly_smiling_face:
