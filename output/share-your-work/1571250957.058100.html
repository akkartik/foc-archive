# Parris 2019-10-16 11:35
Hi all, just launched some new features at https://storyforj.com/ .... AND we're now in public beta (this means you can sign up and try the platform out). If you're looking for something analogous to a stateful interactive doc or non-linear presentation this would be the tool for you.

Anyways, would really love feedback on the tool itself, and the way we're describing ourselves. We've described ourselves as a "simple app builder", "micro-experience creator" and "interactive presentation" before. It seems that in FoC land it's actually quite difficult to describe to end users what you're building. We've found that a seemingly straightforward descriptions like "app builder" to us, often means nothing to an end user.
