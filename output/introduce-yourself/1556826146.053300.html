# Al Mo 2019-05-06 09:59
The motivation was that I was writing very simple programs, i.e. some pure functions with no dependencies. So the only thing I plugged in are pretty much a malloc and some strncpy/strlen equivalents. Dropping emscripten for that kind of thing was overkill, also I always want to learn how things work from scratch. I generally dislike "frameworks" built on top of other things (jQuery, express, you name it).
# Al Mo 2019-05-06 10:00
I have a very dogmatic approach to software development where I want to keep things minimal and with a "bootstrap yourself" attitude. Of course it's (only a bit) more painful but you actually understand the nuts and bolts of everything and that gives you some kind of "magic" instinct when you are solving problems with that particular tool.
