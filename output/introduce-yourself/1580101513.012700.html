My main interests are in psychology/introspection/meditation, which if programming=thought, then programming=emotions, programming=pain/pleasure (or to be more precise expressions of emotions, expressions of pain, expressions of any psychic content).

So tools for thought could also be tools for emotions. Emotions are just nonverbal thoughts, anyway. I hope all this progress that we have made with tools where you can see your own ideas => can be propagated to tools where you can see your own psychological conflicts and problems.

Art has been doing this for millennia (representing our psyche), why can't interactive computing environments do it? Anyhow glad to join
