# Ivan Reese 2019-07-26 10:10
:satellite_antenna: I had the pleasure of guest-hosting the most recent episode of the Future of Coding podcast — an interview with Berlin-based Lisp programmer Jack Rusher. I invite you to listen here [https://futureofcoding.org/episodes/041] or in your podcast player of choice.

Topics: Scheme and Racket and Clojure (oh my), the minimalist but powerful literate editor https://maria.cloud, practical lessons about presentation and feel that programming tool authors can learn from video games and creative coders (like Devine Lu Linvega), a bit about typing disciplines and model checking, graphic design principles, visual programming, and some of our favourite papers.

Enjoy, do let me know what you think. This is my first time (ever) making a podcast, so I'm sure I've still got a lot to learn.
