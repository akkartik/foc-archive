I'm a Javascript React frontend dev. Right now I'm trying out ideas on the side in a few areas:
• Coloring interfaces. I'm actively working on an experiment for this. This work might be helpful for future app UI design tools. I'll post links on this soon.
• Responsive components. I want to set breakpoints at the component level, not at the page level.
• Random experiments in hierarchical and composable systems.

The hypothesis is that better primitives can unlock a lot of new possibilities, specifically for programming UIs.

Thanks @Steve Krouse for tweeting a link to this. It's very exciting that there's a community of people who are interested in the future of coding.
