# Charlie 2019-05-07 20:56
Hi, I'm Charlie, and I've just released the Strat Cloud Language ( https://strat.world/ ). Strat is this odd thing that connects functions together within the cloud and lets users represent services and events at a very high level. I've just found my way here and I'm starting to poke around. As silly as it may be, I believe Strat, or other languages like it will play a big part in "the future of coding". Let me know what you think! Also, I built Strat's compiler in flat, (mostly) dependency-less Javascript, so if you want some deranged hot takes and poor technology evaluations, I'm your guy.
