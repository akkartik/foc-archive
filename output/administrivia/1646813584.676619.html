# Ivan Reese 2022-03-09 19:19
Apologies if I seemed disingenuous. The name was arrived at as part of a huge thread a few years ago that considered a ton of alternatives. The multiple interpretations are deliberate and why the name was chosen.
• Yes, it's a playful pun on sayings of the form "present company ___"
• It is meant to imply discussion about the present of programming as opposed to the future of programming.
• The this is a place to talk about your company meaning was also known and intended at the time the name was picked, so that we wouldn't need a separate channel for job listings, startup news, etc.
There was probably more playful [re/mis]interpretation done by various folks at the time, but it's escaped my memory.
# Ivan Reese 2022-03-09 19:24
In the past, we'd occasionally get someone asking for a #jobs or #hiring or #startups or #business channel. A dedicated channel for #jobs or whatever would be too low-volume for a small community like ours. I'd reply that #random was the place for that — but the goofiness of #random made it awkward (to the best of my recollection; there might have been other failings of #random for this purpose, too).

So when we picked the new channel names, #present-company was specifically intended to be that channel.
