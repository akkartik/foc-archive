# Kartik Agaram 2019-12-12 08:39
I definitely don't intend to exclude spreadsheets. Powerpoint prototypes I haven't heard of. Do you mean like slightly interactive mockups?

I think programming activities lie on a spectrum based on how much thought they require for testing (making one's intent precise, and encoding this precise intent) and design (being conscious of assumptions and trade-offs, and encoding them so they can be revisited). Neither attribute intrinsically privileges text mode, even if all the ways we currently scale along these axes currently rely on text.
