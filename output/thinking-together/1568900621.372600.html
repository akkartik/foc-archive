# Kartik Agaram 2019-09-19 10:11
To elaborate on the two sides: there's a time when a project needs a couple of years alone with its creator (as @Michael Dubakov said), and that works best in a large company managing a portfolio of products (like Xerox). But there comes a time when a project needs execution, and that works best in a company that's all-in on it, providing a sense of urgency and consequence.

So it's not so much that people are good at one or the other, it seems to me. It's the company culture that sets the environment to encourage one phase or another.
