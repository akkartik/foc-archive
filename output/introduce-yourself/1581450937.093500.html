# Joe Johnston 2020-02-12 00:20
Hey Eddy. I love your approach.

Who is it for:
We're targeting developers and technical entrepreneurs.

Value add:

- We're open source, and more flexible than other platforms. With Budibase, we take away a lot of the grunt work similar to other platforms, but we also offer users the flexibility to add snippets of code within different areas of the platform (such as events) which we feel makes it more powerful.

- We're component based, allowing users to choose from material / bootstrap components (and hopefully others in the future).

- Users can host wherever they please. We're working on releasing the builder and we hope to add a hosting and analytics platform in the following months but we feel users should have a choice when it comes to their data.

Budibase is closer to Bubble than Airtable. Users can build a complete Web App including their backend, frontend, and State, all within Budibase. I feel Budibase offers a better dev experience for building web apps, and I would love the opportunity to explain this more over a call if you (or anyone else) like?
