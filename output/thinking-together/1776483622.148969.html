# Konrad Hinsen 2026-04-18 22:56
BTW, a warning message to everyone who dreams of abundance: both ecology and sociology say that abundance breeds competition. In an ecosystem, or a social structure, where there are just enough resources for everyone to survive, individuals help each other to survive in such a way as to use the available resources best. When there is abundance, some individuals grab the excess and derive power over others from it. In human history, it's agriculture that created the abundance that made hierarchical dominance structures possible. Which then got a turbo boost from fossil fuels.
