# Tiago Simoes 2019-03-13 05:14
Hi people from the future,

I'm based in Portugal and I've started working at OutSystems 18 years ago, where we are approaching this opportunity space more from a business side, targeting medium-large companies. Historically it's been hard, as there has been a lot of push-back from the more technical crowd.

Recently industry analysts (Gartner, Forrester) have discovered there is a big market for these low-code solutions (thanks, in part, to our own effort), and we're riding that wave and have become an Unicorn.

We've always approached development from a user experience angle, and now we have more and more established usability practices that steer our IDE and our language.

Looking forward to learn and share, and for the critique of a group that has this subject in their hearts and minds.

We have a free version that can be tried out at https://www.outsystems.com/
