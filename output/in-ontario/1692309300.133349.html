I’m in Toronto. To my knowledge there are no meetups labelled “FoC”, but related meetups that I’m aware of include:
• Toronto Lisp (mostly about: alien technology, robotics, Racket, Scheme, Common Lisp, drawware, Rebol, Forth, strangeness, game jams, etc.), monthly, online (most attendees are outside of Toronto, incl. one from Silicon Valley (everyone welcome))
• CompSci Cabal, Thursday nights reading tech papers, reading free tech books, occasional fire-pit in-person meets
• robotics, rPI, CEO, JS, DevOps, etc. meetups
• Toronto was/is the hub of FBP, but the FBP meetups are now defunct (there is a Discord for FBP)
• Geoffrey Hinton/Vector Institute used to provide free lectures with free lunches
more details available as best as I can provide them...
