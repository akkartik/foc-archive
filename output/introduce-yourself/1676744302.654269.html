# Ivan Reese 2023-02-18 12:11
For folks wondering (as I did) what the Rands Leadership Slack is about, it looks like it's the Slack for Rands in Repose.

For folks who don't know Rands in Repose, it's a blog that is, somewhat, about the management of software teams. But it's also about a bunch of other stuff. It's one of the great blogs (like Kottke and Daring Fireball) that has been running since the earliest days of blogging.

For folks who do know of Rands (the blog) and Michael Lopp (the person), you might also know of Merlin Mann. I will miss no opportunity to mention that Merlin is a huge inspiration, and his ideas about podcasting are foundational in my approach to the medium.
