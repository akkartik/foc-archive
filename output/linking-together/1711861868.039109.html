# Fredrik 2024-04-01 00:02
I would be interested in a critical review of the category and not only a bunch of links to landing pages designed by marketers. I'm having difficulty deciding whether this is a good paradigm or just a gimmick that looks good in screenshots. All these apps are basically reinventing Douglas Engelbart's NLS, but with an infinite scroll of plain paper instead of views and hyperlinks. Engelbart's paradigm is generally considered a good one, and replacing plain paper with views that can dynamically change and present the same information in more than one way is considered a significant step for humanity.

In my experience, having to lay things out on a canvas introduces the overhead of managing the layout, and doesn't use space efficiently enough to make things that are nearly as complex as can easily be made in non-spatial formats. A canvas of nodes is supposed to be a helpful way to look at complex things, but in reality, a canvas of nodes can't handle things that are complex.
