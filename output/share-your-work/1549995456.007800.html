# Humberto Ayres Pereira 2019-02-12 10:17
hello :wave:

we just launched dashdash in Public Beta (dashdash.com)! we're a new online spreadsheet where anyone can create their custom tools. you just type type regular spreadsheet functions and can get call webservices with data & APIs, and do automation, right from the cells.

our Public Beta means we're releasing accounts and features faster now. the tool is stable enough for operating out of it.
What I want: feedback x1000! if you are interested in automations, services and spreadsheets, please register (dashdash.com) and send me all your thoughts and ideas.

if you want to know more about how we are structuring computation inside cells, you can read here: https://medium.com/dashdash/the-5-superpowers-of-spreadsheet-cells-2b44b1455647
