# Andrew F 2023-05-17 12:04
I can add ko-fi.com and gumroad.com to the list, if you didn't already count them under "etc".

Everything I've heard from people who are on those sites indicates that it's very hard to make non-negligible money on them. Only a very small fraction of your audience will ever give you money. Incidentally, I've heard from those same sources that it's preferable to have a lot of low-paying patrons (or whatever) than a few big ones, so that your revenue stream is smoother. Either way you need a pretty big audience.
