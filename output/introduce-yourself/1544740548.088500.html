# Steve Krouse 2019-02-25 02:33
Reactive cyclical dependencies are tricky, but original Conal Elliot FRP figured them out. (You don't have to worry about the detail of how it works, but basically it works the same way recursive functions work - as a fixpoint.) I recently implemented the temperature converter (as part of the 7GUIs examples) with a JS FRP library and made a photo to explain what's going on.

Warning: I expect it'll be quite difficult to understand this stuff if you are new to "original Conal Elliot FRP". It took me ~1 year to fully get it, and still I often get confused.
