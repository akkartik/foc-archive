# Yonatan 2025-08-19 12:51 #
Hello Folks,
The first global sync conference is coming to SF on November 12.

:earth_americas: Get your early bird tickets today: https://syncconf.dev/

For folks not tracking the movement, sync-based technologies are why Superhuman, Figma, Notion, and Linear stand above other products.
Lately, we are seeing sync emerge as the natural pattern for Human & AI collaboration. Sync systems turn complicated challenges like token streaming, long-running tasks, and resumable milestones into simple reactive primitives.

The future of app development is here!
-Enjoy!
