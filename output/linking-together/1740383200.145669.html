# Christopher Shank 2025-02-23 23:46
"Out of Time: Reflections on the Programming Life." by Ellen Ullman (1995)

This series a short stories captures some of Ellen's experiences and observations about working in computing. It touches on both her struggles of being a woman in computing and quite humorous observations about computing. I'd consider it a good "further reading" to the latest FOC episode.

https://gandt.blogs.brynmawr.edu/files/2009/03/ullman.pdf
