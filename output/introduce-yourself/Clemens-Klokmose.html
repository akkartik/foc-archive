# Clemens Klokmose 2018-12-11 04:19
Hi! I am Clemens Klokmose. I'm interested in how to make programming in some form or the other part of everyday computing, and in how to break out of the traditional application silos. I'm a computer scientist by training and working as a professor at department of digital design and information studies at Aarhus University, where I teach students with a humanities background programming. I'm also in charge of the Webstrates project (webstrates.net).
