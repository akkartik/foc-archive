# Ivan Reese 2020-11-16 09:35
I'd be happy with either of the following:

1. #two-minute-week is short weekly update videos only. Everything else goes in #share-your-work.
2. #two-minute-week is short weekly updates. Video, text, audio — you choose the medium. Longer updates, or discussion of past work, goes in #share-your-work.
Both of these keep the name #two-minute-week relevant. Other changes to the format (allowing longer updates, or posts about older work, or more frequent updates) would strain the name, which seems needless — we have #share-your-work as a fallback, so there's no harm in keeping #two-minute-week narrowly defined.

My preference is probably just to keep things as they are, since that's less of a make-work project. But I don't have strong feelings either way.
