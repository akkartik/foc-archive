# Kevin Moore 2020-07-15 15:11
Hello! My name is Kevin Moore. I’m a sometimes software engineer, occasionally speak at Python conferences. My most interesting work is social good/political type of stuff, e.g. I was founding CTO of upsolve.org and I help moderate SandersForPresident on reddit. I mostly view code as a tool to accomplish specific real-life objectives, though I do enjoy sharpening the coding knife. I run r/LearnJavaScript on reddit, too, and have been idly working on a book about breaking into the coding industry (free at https://ZeroToCode.today, people with CS degrees may shudder at some of the commentary). I’ve also been teaching myself to draw, so the book has a lot of very good amateur illustrations.
