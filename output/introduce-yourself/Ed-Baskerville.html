# Ed Baskerville 2019-10-24 15:33
Hi everybody! I’m yet another child of HyperCard hoping to recapture the dream of the ’90s. I’m currently working with @Peter Abrahamsen on an end-user thing; we’re experimenting with building it using a simple Datalog-like model.
Divergently, I’ve spent a lot of time in academia building ecological and evolutionary models, all the while dreaming about more natural ways for scientists to work with computers. (And doing basically nothing about it.)
Less concretely, I’m generally hopeful for a future where many more people view computers as infinitely flexible instruments of creative expression rather than devices limited by what professional software developers are willing to provide.
I’m also interested in how power structures in society have affected the way we use computers—and what we can do about it.
I’m here for the conversations!
