# James Koppel 2018-09-24 13:47
@Joel Jakubovic While getting out of there may be the right idea long-term, if you want to focus on improving your current situation, there’s a lot you can do with communication and precision. Your coworkers may be more reasonable than you realize.

Many people who shoot down proposals prematurely will respond better if you find common ground and make them want you to succeed, if you approach them 1-on-1 and get input early, etc. Those who jump to “this is overengineering” can be convinced if you can explain exactly what’s involved and exactly what future scenarios each choice will help/fail in, especially if you have a track record of successful prediction; one of my clients reported this happening.

On overengineering: I recommend “What is Overengineering” by Max Kanat-Alexander.
