# Doug Moen 2020-01-10 09:33
The quote is attributed to Alan Perlis, but maybe he was quoting someone else. "One can't proceed from the informal to the formal by formal means." From "Epigrams in Programming" by Alan Perlis, ACM SIGPLAN Notices, September 1982.
# Jake Donham 2020-01-10 09:37
iirc David Mackenzie talks about this in Mechanizing Proof; Perlis felt that mechanized proof proceeded from the informal to the formal by formal means.
