Did you see PANE? http://joshuahhh.com/projects/pane/

It’s a kind of visual syntax for normal functional programming with let-items and expressions. One difference from normal “boxes and arrows” is that data visually flows into one destination and any splits are via named variables (just like let-items and expressions)
