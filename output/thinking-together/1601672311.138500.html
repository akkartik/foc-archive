# Lucian Ursu 2020-10-03 02:14
what comes to my mind:
when the programmers works on a piece of code, they are working with 3 views or layers: syntax, semantics, domain-specific knowledge.

At the syntax level are the keywords, the spaces or brackets, the specific symbols that they have to chain in the right way for the parser to make sense of.

At the semantic level they are working with the nodes of the AST. They are creating, modifying or destroying a conditional, a pattern matching expression, reference to a variable, a literal, etc.

At the domain-specific level, they are working with the rules that govern a system. You authenticate something, like a User, you commit or rollback Transaction . these are your types or classes and the operations they support
