# Dan Cook 2019-04-26 19:40
I think most of us here feel similarly. This is a good place to exchange ideas, feedback, and resources. Most of us have found inspiration by Alan Kay and/or Bret Victor, which is a great place to start if you are interested in the idea of getting out of the programming tar-pit you describe. Find Alan Kay on YouTube (e.g. "computer revolution hasn't happened yet" or "programming and scaling"), and Bret Victor at worrydream.com. There's also since good write-ups at vpri.org.

Also, checkout the Future of Coding podcasts
