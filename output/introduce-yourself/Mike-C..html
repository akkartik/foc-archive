Hello, I’m Mike. I’m a graduate student and my research interest is end-user programming for robots and interactive systems more broadly https://homes.cs.washington.edu/~mjyc/. Previously, I mainly worked with block-based visual programming interfaces but recently I’ve been fascinated with the idea of solver-aided programming https://emina.github.io/rosette/. So lately, I’ve been thinking about making solver-aided programming more accessible to non-programmer or novice programmers. Nice to meet you all!
