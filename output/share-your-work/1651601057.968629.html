# William Taysom 2022-05-03 19:00
I like Dragonbox Elements https://dragonbox.com/products/elements. It's not about geometry, not really; but it does two related things:
(1) Categorizing objects based on their properties e.g. a quadrilateral is a figure with four strait sides, in a trapezoid one pair of sides is parallel, in a parallelogram both pairs of sides are parallel.
(2) Constraint propagation e.g. line a is parallel to line b, line b is parallel to line c, so line a is parallel to line c.
