# Ivan Reese 2021-03-07 10:29
Tangent: You (all) may be interested in the video game A Slower Speed of Light, and the related discussion. The game gives you an interactive way to explore the question, "What would it look like if the speed of light were slowed down to a walking pace?" In the game, you need to collect a bunch of tokens (like coins in Mario). Each time you collect a token, the speed of light is slowed down. This slower speed of light leads to all sorts of changes in perception, including changes in color. It's worth playing to experience firsthand, and then reading more about.
