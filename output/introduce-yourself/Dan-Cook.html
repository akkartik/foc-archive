# Dan Cook 2019-09-18 23:17

-> subscribed to the VPRI mailing list

-> I made the SomethingNew GitHub repo as a place to collaborate and catalog our ideas
-> people kept adding resources and links, and we started stumbling across other online collections and communities (like Ivan Reese's)
-> Eventually wound up here, but didn't do much and kept wandering and forgot about it
-> Eventually found Dark, and Ellen Chisa (CEO) referred me back to here
-> Discovered that many other individuals and groups I had come across previously (big names and small) and whom I never thought were connected or that I'd come across again ... Many of them are part of FOC, so I concluded that this is some nexus of it all and became active here
