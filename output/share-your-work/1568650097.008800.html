# Ian Rumac 2019-09-16 14:05
Here’s my opinion:
Good:
- the canvas in the editor, nice! love how quick it is to write new functions, awesome!
- the direct ast editing part - love it, working, working on the same thing, so much autocomplete can be done here!
- The language - seems pretty fun, would love to take a peek at that.

Bad:
- General UI/UX of the editor seems kinda clunky - feels like there is not enough information displayed but it takes up so much space I kinda feel guilty for not seeing the information, making me feel irritated. I’d suggest scratch and start over IMO, find a way to organize information differently.
- The convention over configuration paradigm you’re setting. Configuration isn’t evil. It just isn’t necessary.
- The whole no self-hosting/closed source politics are really surprising, not sure I’d ever try or could recommend to anyone a language like that.
- Also I assume you’re doing a lot of this to collect enormous amounts of data, and I can assume why, but this isn’t the way tbh.


Hope I can beat you to market with my open source product, it’s gonna be a fun competition! :smile:
