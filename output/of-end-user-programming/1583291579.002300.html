# Sol Bekic 2020-03-05 01:50
@Jonathan Edwards the FabAcademy holds weekly lectures and review meetings in one big zoom call with ~60 connected sites and 150 students total. It works pretty well, the rules are simple: connect 5-10min prior to the start and do a sound-check if you want. Once the 'real call' starts, everyone has to stay muted unless they actively want to talk. People with unmuted noisy mics are either reminded or force-muted by the moderator(s). For questions, comments etc the text chat and the 'raise hand' feature can be used. For the FabAcademy anyone may also speak up / interrupt for questions at any time, but this may be a less appropriate policy for a conference.
