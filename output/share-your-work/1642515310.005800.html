# Yousef El-Dardiry 2022-01-18 06:15
On my journey learning about CRDTs and building local-first software, I just published a new open source library; Matrix-CRDT.

Matrix-CRDT connects Yjs with Matrix.org. Basically, you can connect your client-side data store to Matrix, and instead of chat messages, it sends an event stream of data model updates (CRDT updates) to Matrix.

It’s now on the frontpage of HN, so get it while it’s hot :wink: Feedback welcome!
