# Edward de Jong 2020-02-28 13:05
Dreams is the current high water mark for 3D user generated content. It allows people to share art assets, which is the major impediment to user-generated 3D games. Typically the programmers are way outnumbered on AAA projects by staffing for art and music, and so this Dreams system (from Media Molecule in UK) is an amazing system. Roblox is a joke compared to this, and I can't imagine why Roblox has a valuation of 4 billion when Dreams is so far beyond it.
