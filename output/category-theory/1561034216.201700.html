@Cedric Cogell is (+) a function that maps integers to integers though seeing as it takes two elements? The groupish way model addition on integers is as a single object with an arrow for each integer. (+) serves as the composition operation and 0 is the identity. Similarly you get a different monoid (not a group because missing inverses) for (*) and 1.
