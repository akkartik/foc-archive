# nilix 2022-11-09 12:15
Hi I'm Derek (aka nilix); I discovered the FoC podcast a few days ago and thought I might feel right at home in this community!
I'm interested in permacomputing, UI/UX (and of course DevI/DevX) and how we can make them better, and computers as art and tools both for art and for 'practical' things. My website is https://nilfm.cc

My recent projects include a modular CMS (modular in that it can accept multiple adapters around the headless SSG doing the page generation, although right now only one adapter is implemented - https://nilfm.cc/nirvash.html), a simple slippy map annotation tool (https://nilfm.cc/onyx.html), and I'm currently working on a lightweight virtual tabletop for remote D&D. I'm also into the uxn ecosystem, having developed the xrxs experimental uxn game server (https://nilfm.cc/xrxs.html) and worked on a couple other uxn-adjacent projects. I've got a xrxs-capable game in the very early stages but I keep getting too distracted by other projects to make much progress :p

As far as my DevX goes, my general setup is voidlinux with plan9port on top of it, with heavily customized acme and rio forks (https://nilfm.cc/acme9k.html, https://nilfm.cc/ryudo.html). I love shell scripting and making simple (sometimes maybe not so much?) solutions to esoteric problems :)

Glad to be a part of this community, and I expect I may see some familiar faces from the uxn and merveilles crowds :>
