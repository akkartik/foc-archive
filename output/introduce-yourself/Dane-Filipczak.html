# Dane Filipczak 2021-03-20 09:42
Hey there, I’m Dane Filipczak my background is in music technology and functional programming. I’m located in Seattle, and found this community through researching project Eve. I’m working on making a new environment for music composition that includes computation and expressive transformation as a first class citizen, to replace the extremely static and usually frustratingly inexpressive world of MIDI-based DAWs and traditional notation editors. Looking forward to expanding my horizons and learning from what others are attempting in similar areas. Nice to meet you!
