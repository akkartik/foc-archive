Hello everyone, I am the founder of Postman (www.getpostman.com). I am super passionate about development easier, faster, and more accessible to everyone. # Abhinav Asthana 2018-11-26 10:17
@Jesse Aldridge That’s interesting! Typically what happens is the reverse (people going from curl to Postman because they couldn’t get curl to work). We also have a curl importer that tries to translate the command into a Postman request. Could you ask her to try that? Over the past 5 years of building Postman, I have realized that debugging an API call is actually a pretty hard problem with so many layers of technology involved. I read Mindstorms a while back and been trying to use that to guide users on how they can better understand what’s going on and peel these layers back.
