# Massimiliano Gubinelli 2021-05-02 08:43
Hi all. I'm a mathematician and computer languages enthusiast, interested in topics going from formal proofs, user interfaces for editing technical documents, manageable and open source software engineering, high-quality computer typography. I'm part of a group of people developing the free scientific editing platform TeXmacs (www.texmacs.org, https://twitter.com/gnu_texmacs) which is a complete solution for scientific document preparation and reproducible science including interactive sessions with external software. I'm here to look for possible collaborations/interactions with other projects which look forward into the evolution of our way to use computers to express, develop and publish ideas.
