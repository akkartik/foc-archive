# Stefan Lesser 2018-12-20 08:07
Several people here said that they don't like Slack and several others mentioned that they don't know Slack — maybe it's important to make that distinction?
If you don't know Slack: You can subscribe to any thread by clicking the three dots and selecting "Follow Thread" and will then receive notifications (depending on your notification settings) and new messages will also show up in the "New Threads" channel. When you reply in a thread, you will automatically be subscribed to it (and it's as easy to unsubscribe; same process). Works the same on mobile, you get into a thread or create one by tapping a message. This is how I try to deal with volume. Tools > Process.
