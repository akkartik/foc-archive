# Kartik Agaram 2019-06-13 11:59
I just had a minor :bulb: moment about Rebol and Red. I never understood their notion of "Relative Expressions" which they keep trying to compare with S-expressions (Symbolic Expressions). But I think I understand now: basically what they have is an extensible syntax for literals in the language. And they use it for everything. Most interesting. Key is to let the parser for each literal syntax decide when to evaluate it.
