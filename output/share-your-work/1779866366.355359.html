# Konrad Hinsen 2026-05-27 04:18
You have to know quite a bit about LLMs to do a good job - as with other powerful tools. And it would help a lot if we had more transparent LLMs, whose learning set could be scrutinized.

Perhaps the two biggest issues with LLMs, other than their huge negative externalities, are opacity and the exceptional ease for using them badly.

# Konrad Hinsen 2026-05-28 02:08
Just about everything possible is happening with LLMs in science at the moment. Many people use them as tools to help them with their research, or just with writing their reports. Some check the output, others don't. There are plenty of scientific publications with hallucinated references, or telltale phrases such as "Certainly, here is the paragraph you asked for:". Publications completely written by LLMs (based on no human research work at all) remain very rare, but a few made it into some conference proceedings.
