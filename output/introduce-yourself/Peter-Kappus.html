Hi everyone! I’m Peter and I’ve been noodling with technology since my parents got a Mac Plus in 1984 and I started making little games in Quick Basic. This has morphed into 1 year of a computer science degree which then became an architecture degree (as in buildings, not software), and a career doing web development and more recently agile coaching, portfolio management, digital transformation and DevOpsy stuff.

Generally I love helping digital teams to get clear on what they’re doing and why and then get better at doing those things while having more fun.

Recently I’ve been delighted to discover Nudel and Pastagang and all the things that go with it!

Professionally you can learn more about me here. Creatively, I post algorithmic art and other things as “Abstraktor” and I perennially debate how to attract mainstream clients while also sharing my more niche passions and experiments under the same “brand”.

Excited to meet you all!
