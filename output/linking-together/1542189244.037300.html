# Stefan Lesser 2018-11-14 01:54
It seems obvious that the person who knows most about the system needs to document it. However, it turns out good documentation often comes from other people, who had to understand the system from scratch long after it has been designed.

It’s hard to look at our own creations with a Beginner’s Mind again, but that’s really helpful, if not essential, to write good documentation.

And I’m not sure if writing documentation itself can be more fun — I’d like to think so. For sure though, there are people who like writing documentation. So it’s great if you have a chance to collaborate with those.
