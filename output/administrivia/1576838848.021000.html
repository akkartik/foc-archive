# nicolas decoster 2019-12-21 03:23
Zulip doesn't have threads in the sense of Slack threads. But streams are meant to address this need. Usually, when you start a new discussion (like sharing a link, asking for feedback for your last pet project, etc.) you create a subject on the corresponding channel, and discussions start on this specific stream (# feedback > "my pet project"). There is also a view for those who prefer to have a "general stream" whith all discussions mixed with messages in chronological order, a bit like old Slack without threads.
