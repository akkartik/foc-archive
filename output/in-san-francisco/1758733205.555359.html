# Josh Bleecher Snyder 2025-09-24 17:36
I can offer better advice if you can say more about what kinds of this you like to do in cities. But some generic "SF tourist" advice:

• the Sutro Baths are pretty and worth a walk around
• best secret view of the city is Tank Hill, de Young viewing tower also good, avoid Twin Peaks
• take a Waymo, it's fun
• avoid Fisherman's Wharf
• Cable Car Museum is free and surprisingly neat
• best beach is probably Baker Beach (note: nude at one end; swimming is intermittently dangerous, if you like swimming in frigid waters, go to Aquatic Park instead)
• Dolores Park on the weekend is a scene
• my favorite "just wander around" neighborhood is Mission/Castro, although I'm biased
• good burritos at Papalote (La Taqueria also good, get crispy tacos, but very long lines)
• fancier vegetarian dining with nice views: https://www.greensrestaurant.com
• do not leave anything in your car unattended, anywhere in the city, even for a short time, even hidden under a seat.
• Dandelion Chocolate is touristy but really good chocolate. Bi-Rite Ice Cream has best ice cream around. Tartine has remarkable pastries.
There's plenty more where that came from. Happy to answer questions, expound more--just lmk what you want to know.
