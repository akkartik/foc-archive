# David Broderick 2019-05-02 08:19
Hi everyone, I'm David Broderick. Originally from northern California, I spent 20 years in Europe (mainly Switzerland, Czech Republic and most recently southern France) and I'm currently living in Boise, Idaho.
I used to be part of the 2020 group in San Francisco which has similar discussions and is paired with the Future of Programming meetups in the Bay Area (https://www.meetup.com/Future-of-Programming). My big, long-term project is called Kayia which is an attempt to layer the spreadsheet model onto a declarative language, if that makes any sense. I would link to the site for that, but since it's undergoing a lot of changes, it just has a stub for now. I'm looking forward to joining in on the discussions!
