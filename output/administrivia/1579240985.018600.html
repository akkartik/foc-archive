The Recurse Center has a “Blaggregator” — a tool where you can register your blog’s RSS feed and then every new post will automatically be announced in a #blogging Zulip channel.
https://blaggregator.recurse.com/about/

1. I think that would be great for us too so we can track and discuss blog posts of members here. I don’t know how to make that work but surely there must be some Slack app that can do this.
2. That could be also interesting for automatically posting tweets here although I would like that to be a different channel to distinguish blog posts from tweets.
