# Doug Moen 2019-10-24 14:21
The Y combinator. Y f = f (Y f) or Y = λf.(λx.(f (x x)) λx.(f (x x))). Scary because: (1) can be used to demonstrate that untyped lambda calculus is unsound as a deductive system; (2) causes an infinite loop when applied to most functions; (3) single letter variable names.
