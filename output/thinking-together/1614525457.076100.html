For something new, I'd start with RDF. The conceptual building block is simple (subject-predicate-object), which makes it pretty flexible. They now also have SPARQL - a query language for RDF. I'd pick RDF over any relational or algebraic schema models. I think it is closest to the conceptual level of most subjects.
