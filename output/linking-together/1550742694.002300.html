# Nick Smith 2019-02-21 18:24
SOLID is about control and ownership of your own data, so yeah people need to rent their own servers to host their data. The only alternative to this would involve a decentralised data storage network.

The SAFE Network has plans to integrate the principles of SOLID (if it ever becomes a thing) https://safenetwork.org/

Hopefully if/when other blockchain-ey projects become practical they will support it as well.
