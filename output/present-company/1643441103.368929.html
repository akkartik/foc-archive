# Tom Larkworthy 2022-01-29 00:12
Hi, I feel very annoyed that UX generally takes about 50% of the energy of any project. My fasted development is now using observable and I have a hierarchical system that helps me. Maybe it is interesting to you? I like Observable because there is zero environment and the cell model is simpler than the full DOM model.

My method for speeding up UI development on Observable makes use of reusing other people's GUI libraries as much as possible with the escape hatch of writing your own. Having d3 inbuilt means you can basically do anything and you can walk up and down the ladder of abstractions easy. Cons: is JS.

https://observablehq.com/@tomlarkworthy/ui-development
