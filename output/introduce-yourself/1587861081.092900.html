# Boarders 2020-04-26 11:22
Overall I didn't like the feedback cycle I had with research mathematics which was a lot of feeling very confused and like I was not making much tangilbe progress. Programming in many ways feels just the opposite to me - you get quite immediate feedback all the time. I was also not very good at the kind of research I was trying to do which was rather technical. On top of that I simply got the haskell bug, I didn't know about functional programming (my programming experience consisted to that point of a Java course from university) and I found it to fit how I think really well.

I have watched both of those Youtube channels, they are quite delightful.
