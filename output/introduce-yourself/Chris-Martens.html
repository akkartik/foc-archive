Hello everyone! My pronouns are they/them. I direct research on games/narrative/interactive systems and teach computer science at NC State. I spend a lot of time thinking about making better tools for game development & other forms of expressive computing, combining Programming Languages and HCI approaches. This includes how language design (models of computation) influence how creators think about creative possibility spaces, and about how game design and HCI principles can make programming a more playful, expressive, and joyful experience.
