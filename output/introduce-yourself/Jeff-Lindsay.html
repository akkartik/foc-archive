Hey everyone! I'm Jeff. Thought I'd try a new online community and this seemed like a good one. Like a lot of you, I assume, I think about this topic a lot. Maybe too much. If there's any Plan 9 curious people, we're hosting an online hangout / watch party for Plan 9 stuff in my quiet little Discord tomorrow night. Let me know if anybody wants an invite.
