@Kartik Agaram My mental model of "simplicity" has aspects of "a small number of distinct concepts" and "a minimum of cognitive effort" needed to use a system/accomplish a given task.

I believe many current systems aren't designed with a high priority goal of minimizing mental effort, likely because the designers and intended audience don't see high mental effort as a disadvantage- many even an advantage. Or perhaps the effort extended is invisible because the participants enjoy it.
