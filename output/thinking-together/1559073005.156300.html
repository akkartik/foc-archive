The main problem that Pharo beginners face today is that all books and courses are based on outdated versions of Pharo. The current stable release, Pharo 7, looks quite a bit different from Pharo 5 that is used in the MOOC. The differences are superficial, mainly esthetical, but still disturbing for newcomers.
