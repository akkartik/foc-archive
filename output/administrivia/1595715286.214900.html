Suggestions welcome. It'd be nice to have something that didn't rely on a Slack plugin. Perhaps...

• At the start of the month, someone posts to the channel something like "Anyone who wants to do a 1-on-1 this month, reply with your @username"
• A week later, someone copies all the replies, uses a regex to grab the @names, and throws them into a simple script or JS page that randomly pairs them up.
