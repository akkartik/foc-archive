# Marcin Ignac 2020-11-17 05:32
Hi!

Together with Damien Seguin and @Nick Nikolov we have been building a node-based creative-coding tool at http://variable.io that is JavaScript based and npm friendly: Nodes.

We have used it for all our creative coding, generative art and datavis projects for the last 3 years and it’s really changed how we think with code. If you are interested in knowing more and trying it out, we just shared an open beta today for everyone: https://twitter.com/nodes_io/status/1328666419890954241

You can download it on our website: https://nodes.io/

--- end of announcement :slightly_smiling_face:

Happy to engage in any kind of discussion from “Why on earth JS?” to “Did you find unidirectional data flow useful or troublesome?” or even help getting started.

3. We actually do have experimental watch functionality but it’s not part of the main app just yet. Again people wanted this because “I’m so used in VSCode!” (really) so switching to monaco and having autocomplete for core nodes stuff mitigates that a bit
