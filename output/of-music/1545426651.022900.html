# Ivan Reese 2018-12-21 13:10
I've done some live-coding, but I quickly gravitated toward pre-building reactive music systems that I could then play along with in a live setting. I found it made for a more interesting viewing experience for the audience. Rather than being hunched over a laptop, you could move around the stage, change costumes, interact with objects, and generally be much more theatrical, while still having the benefit of computation to do interesting things with the sound. As an example of what one of my performances looked like: http://ivanish.ca/the-unlimited-dream-company/
