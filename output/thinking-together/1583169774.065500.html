# Chris Knott 2020-03-02 14:36
I am very lazy and generally prefer things which are Easy in the Simple vs Easy Rich Hickey sense. Close to hand, fast to get going with, eventual catastrophes :grinning:

I also like tools that know their scope, integrate well with others, and leave their connections "raw". My worst nightmare is things that are highly configurable but in a "closed" way, e.g. a lot of creative software like Photoshop, Blender, Unreal Engine etc.

For example, in Sublime Text, the Build command will basically just execute a string you provide as a command. This is so much more appealing to me to than trying to configure Eclipse. The "connection" in Sublime Text is more "raw".
