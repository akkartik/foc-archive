Hi all, I'm Noah. Gosh, there's a lot of interesting work represented here. I'm an untrained, uneducated freelance web dev in Tennessee who, about 3 years ago, thought: "wouldn't be cool if you could actually see programs?" Since then I've discovered Fred Brooks, Bret Victor, VISSOFT, and, well, Computer Science in general. And recently, @Steve Krouse and the community he has begun to catalogue and connect!
