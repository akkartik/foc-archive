# Seth Hinz 2025-05-13 10:40
Logix looks cool :) I’m still not sure how sold I am on this approach, even though my demo is quite similar :joy:

I think a lot of approaches to node based programming systems are kind of just a different coat of paint on textual programming - there’s usually a very direct translation between the two systems in your head. That leads to kludgy things like arithmetic being painful (Typing ‘3*x-2**y’ is easy, but wiring that up in VR takes at least 3 nodes and 5 wires - it gets worrying close to good cardio)! I’m really interested in exploring oddball stuff that leans into the strengths of these visual, tactile tools… what would programming look like if we never invented keyboards!
