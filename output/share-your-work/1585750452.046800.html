# Steve Peak 2020-04-01 07:16
Now, for more context. Think Star Trek when Pike talks to the computer. He can ask complex questions and create complex procedures with just his voice, but the computer will have a dialog with him to get all the necessary context to achieve what he is asking for. As the idea of Dialog Driven Development is to focus more on the conversation with the user instead diagram/visual development and traditional coding.
# Jared Windover 2020-04-01 07:30
