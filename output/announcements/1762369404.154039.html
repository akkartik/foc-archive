# Ivan Reese 2025-11-05 11:03
This month, in lieu of our usual virtual meetup, we'll be hosting a workshop about Artifact, a new visual programming environment from @Dennis Hansen, creator of Holograph. Similar to the Unit workshop, we've invited Dennis to show us how Artifact is meant to be used, and dig deep into its many unusual and fascinating design choices.

I've been talking to Dennis about visual programming for a few years now, and am excited for more people to see his work. He's one of the few people I know who is really keen to push on the "visual" part of visual programming, with a strong sense of visual design and the urge to imbue tangible objects with programmatic meaning.

Join the workshop live to ask questions and explore the space together, but if you can't make it there will as always be a recording. Big thanks to @Maikel van de Lisdonk for helping coordinate this (it's been months in the making). I'm personally really excited about this one, and hope to see you there!
