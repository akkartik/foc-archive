# Willa Drengwitz 2017-09-26 11:52
I am excited to be there. Of note, I forgot to link any information about the Lean Coffee[1] meeting method that I feel would work really well for this group. The link goes into more depth then what we typically do internally at Reaktor when having a community of practice meeting. The simple method.

1. [5 mins] Personal brainstorming ideas writing to notes
2. Everyone sticks there notes into a big pile
3. A topic is selected as a group
4. [10 mins] Group discussion on topic
5. goto 3

Its simple, yet effective at creating well balanced group discussions. I’m not trying to dictate this as our method, just trying to share. I am open to other types of facilitation methods!

[1] http://agilecoffee.com/leancoffee/
