# Rob Haisfield 2021-03-10 20:47
Hi, I’m Rob Haisfield. I’m a behavioral product strategy and gamification consultant, as well as a behavioral product strategist at Spark Wave. Basically that means that I think about how to influence user behavior such that people gain more value out of the apps that they use.

I’m largely in this group due to my work on the onboarding for GuidedTrack, a domain-specific language for interconnected form building --> web applications. Since working with them, I’ve become fascinated with powerful horizontal products with a smooth learning curve from beginner to power user, and domain-specific languages feel like the next natural step. There’s a lot that could be done to make DSLs learnable, even as replacements for full-blown apps, and I want to help make that happen!
