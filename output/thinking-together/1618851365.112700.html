# William Taysom 2021-04-19 19:27
Been a while. It's not so complicated â€” until it is.

To a first approximation, you clone whatever object looks most like what you want.

Eventually, you'll find that what you meant was that you wanted a copy (independent slots) rather than a clone (inherited slots) at which point, you'll tend to keep a bunch of prototype objects around which have the defaults for everything.

Later, as you start to have complex structures of objects, you'll learn that the right clone/copy semantics interacts with how shallow/deep you want things to be. It gets messy, and the next thing you know, you're writing factory methods. :scream:
